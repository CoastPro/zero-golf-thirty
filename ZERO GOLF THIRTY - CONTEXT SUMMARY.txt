ZERO GOLF THIRTY - CONTEXT SUMMARY
Tech Stack: React, TypeScript, Vite, Tailwind CSS, Supabase, Vercel
Database Tables:

tournaments (name, course_name, course_par[], flights[], hidden, logo_url, sponsor_logo_url, sponsor_logo_2_url, player_instructions, leaderboard_settings)
players (name, handicap, quota, flight, phone, paid, in_skins)
groups (number, starting_hole, starting_position, tee_time, qr_code)
group_players (group_id, player_id, position, cart_number)
scores (player_id, hole, score)
saved_courses, saved_players

Key Features:

Phone login: Players enter last 4 digits → auto-locked to their group
QR codes: /tournament/{id}/score?group={groupId} locks to group
One person scores for entire group
Leaderboards: Gross/Net, Stableford, Skins (reorderable/hideable)
Printable scorecards (landscape, 2 sponsor logos) & cart placards

Important Routes:

/ → AdminLogin (PIN or phone login)
/phone-login → PhoneLogin component
/tournament/{id}/score → ScoringInterface (with ?group= param for restriction)
/tournament/{id}/leaderboard → Leaderboard
/admin/tournament/{id}/players → PlayerManagement

Key Files:

src/pages/ScoringInterface.tsx - Main scoring (restricted by ?group=)
src/pages/PlayerManagement.tsx - Has phone field
src/pages/PhoneLogin.tsx - Last 4 digit login
src/pages/Leaderboard.tsx - Maintains group param
src/types/database.types.ts - All interfaces

Current Status: Fully working and deployed!
